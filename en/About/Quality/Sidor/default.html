Quality and environment

SMP is accredited for inspection and testing and offers help with certification.

Our accreditation shows that we fulfil very high quality requirements.

Our quality system complies with the requirements in ISO 17020 (Inspection) and ISO 17025 (Testing) and is regularly audited by SWEDAC, our national accreditation body. We are also building an Environmental Management-system in accordance with ISO 14001.

Our accreditation - your key to Europe

It is our goal to make it possible for the manufacturer to sell machines freely in the European market, taking a minimum of risk. It is in order to live up to this goal that we are accredited for testing and also a Notified Body ( Notified Body ) in a number of fields.
