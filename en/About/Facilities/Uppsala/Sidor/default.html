SMP Uppsala (Certification)

In Uppsala, we have our certification unit, where we are working to help companies in the CE marking process. We carry out inspections and risk analysis of machines and production lines, educate personal in machinery safety issues and issues certificates that provide the products access to the common market.

Since december 16th SMP in Uppsala shares facilities with the fellow subsidiary company JTI-insitute for Agricultural and Environmental Engineering.
