Welcome to SMP

SMP offers a number of services within the areas of machine- product- and environmental safety. We are accredited for inspection and testing, offer product certification and are also a Notified Body in a number of fields.

Furthermore, we are happy to offer tailored tests, training-programmes and safety-inspections in accordance with the needs of our client's.

Our independence and impartiality constitute a guarantee that our inspection, certification and testing services comply with the highest standards of independence and integrity and thus is free from any external manipulation.

The RISE institutes SMP Swedish Machinery Testing Institute , SP Technical Research Institute of Sweden, Swedish ICT and Innventia are merging in order to become a stronger innovation partner for businesses and society. We will also continue to develop our services within inspection, testing, certification, calibration and verification. At the end of the year we will change our name to RISE.
