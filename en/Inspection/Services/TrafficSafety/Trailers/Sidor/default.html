Trailer

Rules regarding trailers are found in the regulation VVFS 2003:20 as well as the extension VVFS 2007:105.

Download VVFS 2003:20

Download VVFS 2007:105

Registration

Trailer, pulles by a traffic tractor or a M-red class II machine with a curb weight over 2000 kg, shall, when used for person or goods transporting be registered except when the use does not concern:

  • crossing the road

  • Transport exclusively or almost exclusively of products or supplies for agriculture, forestry, commercial crop or commercial fishing (load at forestry may not exceed 15 tons)

  • transport of equipment to be operated with the towing vehicle or goods needed to run this or attachment needed for the driver

    • Trailer for M-red class I and off-road vehicles are always needed to be registered (exceptions: vehicles exclusively used within fenced area). The technical requirements are comparable with those for car trailer. Inspection is required, the first time after three years and thereafter every two years.

    Tax calculated on a registered trailer after the trailer´s permissible gross weight on the road, and the allowable gross weight on the axes. For trailer to M-red class I, used the same way as mentioned above, does need to pay road tax. For vehicles of M-red class I or heavy off-road vehicles over 30 km/h must pay a overload fee.

    Certificate of conformity

    In connection with registrations inspection, requirements on the manufacturer's certificate stating that certain technical requirements are met. Click here to download the document "Registration inspection of trailer" which cover the requirements for the certificate of conformity of trailers.
