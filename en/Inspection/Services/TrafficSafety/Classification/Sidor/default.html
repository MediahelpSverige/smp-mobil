Vehicle classifications

In some cases the assessment of a vehicle classification among the different categories could be complicated.

The classification is based on Act on traffic definitions (SFS 2001:559) and the Transport Agency´s regulations .

In doubtful cases, the Transport Agency can determine the classification of the vehicle.
