Environmental and regulatory records

SMP has developed an Environmental and regulatory journal that is especially suited for construction machinery.

It can be ordered by mailing info@smp.sp.se

You can also order SMP's Corporate brochure and subscribe to ”teknik;forskning” .

Environmental inspection of construction companies

The service "Environmental inspection of construction companies" has been closed down.

The content of the service was based on the different client demands that arrived almost 15 years ago when the debate on environmental awareness started in the construction industry.

Today, the client requirements are fairly uniform and well-known as the common "Environmental requirements ​for procurement and services" was developed. This reduced the interest for our service. It was the largest cities in Sweden, ie Stockholm, Gothenburg and Malmö, who were behind the common requirements. The revised and now current procurement requirements are also developed by the Transport Agency.

The environmental requirements can be downloaded on the website of the Transport Agency:

Download the requirements from the Transport Agency
