Certification | CE marking

SMP Certification guides you through the CE marking process

Whether you manufacture a single machine or need CE marking an entire machine line so we can assist you.

We offer:

  • Counseling
  • Education
  • Risk snalysis
  • Inspection and certification of new machines and machine lines
  • Status Assessment of existing machines and machine lines
  • Assessment of technical Documentsation
  • Type examination of machinery and protective clothing in our role as a notified body
  • Internal production control or verification of single objects according to the noise directive in our role as a notified body

    • The advantage of engaging SMP in your CE markings process is that you get an impartial and competent review of your product or facility.
