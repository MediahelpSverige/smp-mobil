Certification

Safety of machinery and production lines

SMP certification unit works mainly with machinery safety and we offer safety assessments of new machines and machine lines, i.e. such assessments that are necessary for the manufacturers CE marking. We also carry out assessments of work equipment, often machines and machine lines used by manufacturing and processing industry. We have many years experience in the following industries:

  • Workshop
  • Carpentry
  • Mounting
  • Person lift
  • Garden
  • Forestry and agriculture
  • Construction
  • Sawmill
  • Concrete industri
  • Disposal
  • etc.

    • Machine-safety is a question of liability

    The manufacturer is liable for delivered products being CE-marked and also the products fulfilling all safety requirements laid down in EC-directives and standards. Machines and production lines that are used in the production process must also fulfil high safety requirements.

    We know the regulations

    SMPs long experience gives us the swedish leading specialists when it comes to inspect and assess of the risks of machines and production lines whether they are to be CE-marked or not. ​

    When it comes to machinery safety and risk assessments it is very helpful to know which directives, standards, regulations, etc., are aaplicable. That knowledge exists amoung our experts.

    Minimize the risk-taking

    A manufacturer can always minimize his own risk by using the services of a third party. Our experts inspect machinery and production lines as well as technical files.
