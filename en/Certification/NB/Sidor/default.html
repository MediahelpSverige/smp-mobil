SMP as a Notified Body

The European safety directives state that certain products and components must be certified by a third party: a so-called Notified Body. A Notified Body must have expertise knowledge for the products in question and must have a quality-system that fulfils special requirements. SMP is a Notified Body in a number of fields:

The Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC

Wood-working machines and meat-saws

Hand-fed circular-saws, band-saws, planes and cutters intended for working with wood, meat and similar products.

Power chainsaws

All power chain-saws, irrespective of whether they are powered by electricity or by combustion engine.

Presses

Presses for the cold working of metals with manual loading and/or unloading.

Refuse-collection vehicles

Manually-loaded trucks for the collection of household refuse, incorporating a compression mechanism.

Vehicle-servicing lifts

Devices for the lifting of cars and other vehicles.

Devices for the lifting of persons

Devices for the lifting of persons, involving a risk of falling from a vertical height of more than 3 metres.

Safety cabs and bus-bodies (ROPS/FOPS)

All safety cabs and bus-bodies separately marketed.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) 89/686/EEC

Protective clothing for forestry work

Refers to eg protective jackets, gloves, boots and protective trousers.

Noise in the environment 2000/14/EG

Noise from outdoor equipment

The directive lays down requirements for approximately 60 various types of machines that are intended for outdoor use and for which third-party certification applies. SMP's testing laboratories can also perform noise-measurements for the machines in question.
