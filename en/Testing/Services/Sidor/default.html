Testing | Testing services

Our list is long...

We are specialists in a wide range of tests. One of our main specialities is to provide tailored tests to fit our client's needs. The list below shows only some of the testing-services we can offer.

  • Analysis of exhaust emission, as well as power measurement and fuel metering, including engines in the power range 0-400 kW.
  • sound power and sound pressure measurements.
  • Vibration measurements (whole body vibration and hand/arm)
  • Testing strength and durability of safety cabs, for example trucks, construction equipment, tractors, trucks and forestry.
  • Testing strength and durability of bus body sections and the tribes for goods vehicles.
  • Security testing of chainsaws, brush cutters, protective clothing, forestry, lawn & garden equipment, etc.
  • Producer Gas: engine operation and cogeneration.
  • Stability, braking and steering testings on trucks, construction machinery, etc.
  • Environmental Testing of oils (viscosity, hydrolysis, water content, etc.).
  • Security tests of bicycles and bicycle components and certification.
  • Technical service for issue directive 97/68/EC with supplementary directive 2022/88/EG, spark ignition engines.
