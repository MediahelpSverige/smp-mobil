Testing of consumer products

SMP conducts regular testing of consumer products on behalf of manufacturers or consumer magazines such as "Råd & Rön" eller "Ica-Kuriren".

Tests commissioned by manufacturers often include mainly machinery safety. When it comes testings for consumer magazines are also features that ease of use, capacity and performance are important.

Example of products tested by SMP:

  • Lawnmowers
  • Snowblowers
  • Equipment for weed control
  • Bike lights
  • Bicycle Child Seats
  • Hedge trimmers
  • Trimmers, etc.

    • SMP also offers unique facilities for testing of bicycles and bicycle components. ​
