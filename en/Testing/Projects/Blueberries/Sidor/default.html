Cleansing of berries

SMP will carry out a project to look at technology to clean blueberries and cranberries. SMP will develop an experimental machine to try in practical operation.

The idea is that it shall have the capacity to fit those who refines berries in a small scale.

For eg juice, jam or direct to the consumer on a farmers' market.

In the project we would like to come in contact with those who are interested of cleansing of berries or would like to buy cleansed berries. We would like to hear views on what the machine should be capable of and how it should be designed. It is uncertain, but it may be possible to test cleansing during this season though we hopefully will be able to test cleansing soon. ​
