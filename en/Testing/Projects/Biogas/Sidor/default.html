Testing | Projects | Biogas from machinery

Get your machine converted to biogas

SMP will help the Board of Agriculture with a project which will rebuild some machines to biogas as fuel.

Machines are rebuilt with methane diesel technology which means that you can run on only diesel or biogas combined with diesel. Anyone interested can apply via the link below. ​

Whoever interested or wants more information could contact hans.arvidsson@smp.sp.se (+46 (0)10-516 64 72) or read the investigation which SMP has made for the Board of Agriculture by clicking the link below.
